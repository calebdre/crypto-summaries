Xenon is a project that aims to create an alternative blockchain from the EOS project open-source codebase..  In order to reduce the regulatory risks associated with the standard crowdfunding method (Initial Coin Offering) and ensure a widespread distribution of the tokens, Xenon will distribute their tokens through an airdrop..  The Proposed Airdrop: - Total tokens 1,000,000,000 XNN - 70% airdropped: - 300,000,000 XNN proportionally airdropped to all ether addresses with >0.

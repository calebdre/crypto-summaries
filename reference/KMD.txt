Komodo is a privacy-centric cryptocurrency that combines the anonymity of ZCash with the security of Bitcoin.   Using a new consensus mechanism developed by the Komodo team, delayed Proof of Work, Komodo blocks can be notarized using the Bitcoin blockchain.   KMD is issued through an Equihash-based PoW protocol, and the new block information is sent to pre-voted notary nodes

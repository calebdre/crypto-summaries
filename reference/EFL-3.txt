The Electronic Gulden Crypto Currency (EFL) is named after the Gulden Florjin a Dutch coin from the Middle Ages..  Again like SpainCoin, Scotcoin, CryptoEscudo, and other nationally inspired crypto currencies, the Electronic Gulden is a premined coin for distribution to a specific nationality - in this case the Dutch..  50% of the coin can be mined by anyone and 50% will be allotted to Dutch Households and verified using two proofs of address - two utility bills.

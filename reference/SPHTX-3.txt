SophiaTX is a platform that focuses on integrating blockchain with SAP and other major ERP, CRM, and SCM systems..  The platform aims to expand the possibilities of enterprise applications with the trust and security that comes with blockchain technology..  The platform can be thought of as three components: a blockchain designed and built specifically for business environment, a development platform with integration APIs to SAP and other enterprise applications, and a marketplace for both industrial companies and a global community of experts, consultants, and developers to buy and sell apps.

The ETHBIT token is a type of currency used to pay fees on the ETHBITS Local platform (for example buy/sell Bitcoin, Ether and other cryptocurrencies)..  ETHBIT tokens can also be used to distribute dividends to investors..  If you hold ETHBITS tokens, you hold a portion of our platform, and by holding a portion of our platform you are entitled to your share of a distribution of 40% of the platform's profit.

Smart Investment Fund Token (SIFT) is an ERC20-compliant smart contract within the Ethereum blockchain that pays dividends in ether monthly..  Each SIFT represents a single share in a volume-trading investment fund for cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin, Ethereum, Ripple, Dash and Litecoin..  In addition to monthly dividends, shareholders will also be able to trade their tokens at market rates allowing them to cash-out as the fund value increases over time.

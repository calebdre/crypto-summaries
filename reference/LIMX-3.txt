<span>LimeCoinx (LIMX) is an X11 altcoin that is based on the 11 hashing functions of blake, bmx, groestl, jh, keccak, skein, luffa, cubehash, shavite, simd, and echo.  The maximum money supply of LIMX is set to 21 million.  The block time is 5 minutes and the difficulty retargets every 36 blocks using the Dark Gravity Wave process
